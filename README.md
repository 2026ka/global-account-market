# Global Account Market

## English

Global Account Market introduces the concept of digital account trading across different regions and platforms.

With the growth of online services, digital accounts have become important resources for business, marketing, and personal use.

This project provides basic information about account marketplaces and global account usage.

## 中文说明

Global Account Market 介绍全球范围内的账号交易市场及其基本概念。

随着互联网发展，数字账号已成为营销、业务以及个人使用的重要资源。

本项目帮助用户了解账号市场结构及全球账号使用情况。
